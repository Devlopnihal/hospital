About us

MRM Memorial Hospital Pvt. Ltd

The hospital is a nice amalgamation of experienced professionals - Specialists, Consultants, Resident medical officers, GNM nursing staff, Paramedical staff and a large number of other executives and paramedical staff. Backed up by modern and well equipped Recovery, ICU, and dedicated workers in various other non-medical services such as MIS, Accounts, Maintenance, marketing etc. support the goal of the hospital in its own way. Mahesh Ranjan Mohilary (MRM) Memorial Hospital was formally inaugurated by the wife of BTC Chief Mr. Hagrama Mohilary Mrs. Sewli Mohilary in presence of large number of dignitaries. The four storied MRM Memorial Hospital has facilities for Medicine, Surgery, Gynaecology, Child specialist, orthopedics, Eye Specialist etc with ICUs and emergency units.
